Established in 2016 in Nepal, ESOS Global has moved to India Post Covid 19, & is operating in India since then. ESOS GLOBAL stands for Entrepreneur's School of Success Global, where we work with our super talented, creative, enthusiastic and professional team to deliver the best. Our tagline and slogan is "We cultivate entrepreneurs we develop nations, we are ESOS Global". We are pioneers and experts in Entrepreneurship development, leadership development, Personality development and training programs. Our development programs are critical and science-based which are designed and delivered through a process that helps organizations build their capacity to change and achieve greater effectiveness by developing, improving, and reinforcing strategies, structures, and processes. Our development programs are evidence-based and structured process. Our development programs are aimed at organizational & individual effectiveness. It, therefore, has a number of (business) outcomes, they do include financial performance, customer satisfaction, organizational member engagement, and an increased capacity to adapt and renew the organization. Developing, improving, and reinforcing strategies, structures, and processes. Our developmental programs apply to changes in strategy, structure, and/or processes. This implies a system-approach, where we focus on an entire organizational system. This can include the full organization, one or more locations, or a single department.
